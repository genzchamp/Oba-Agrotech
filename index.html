Quick Poultry Health Tips

Newcastle Disease

Highly contagious viral disease. Prevent by isolating sick birds, vaccinating, and maintaining clean pens.

Gumboro Disease

Weakens the immune system of young chickens. Vaccinate and ensure clean water and feed.

Coccidiosis

Parasitic disease causing diarrhea and weight loss. Maintain hygiene, rotate litter, and use anticoccidial feed.
